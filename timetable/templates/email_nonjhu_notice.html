Update on Semester.ly

Dear Semester.ly user,

Starting March 8th, 2019, Semester.ly will be entering a new partnership with Johns Hopkins University’s IT department. We’re excited about this new development and the opportunity to continue making course registration easier, however, this partnership means that we will no longer be supporting schools outside of JHU. While we loved interacting with students around North America, this change will allow us to focus on updating our core platform and meeting course registration deadlines. In the future, we hope to expand our services once again but until then, we will focusing on JHU. To ensure your safety and privacy, we will rid of all user data related to non-JHU students so there is no action needed but we wanted to take a moment to thank you for your support over the years. After careful consideration, we have decided that this partnership will improve the quality of Semester.ly.

Sincerely,
The Semester.ly Team
