Choice of materials

Your instructor has indicated that you can choose which books you want to purchase out of this predefined set. YOU NEED NOT PURCHASE ALL THE ITEMS IN THIS SET. The heading for this material indicates the number of books you should choose, but the choice of which materials to choose is yours.

You should select the materials most relevant to your interests. Your instructor may have additional instructions in the syllabus or course/section notes at the top of the page.

Packages

Your instructor has chosen a package of course material (a bundle of multiple items) for this course, rather than a book or books.

If you do not want to buy the package, you can buy the individual components. If so, please note that your instructor has indicated that certain components are REQUIRED. Those components are marked 'Required'.

Open the 'Shop for individual package components' section to see individual items in the package.

Note: In some cases, not all items included in the package will be listed under 'Shop for individual package components'.

About Digital Providers

The digital course materials we offer are published by different providers. The content of the digital materials will be the same regardless of the provider, but each provider has its own eReader application that you will use to access the content.

Differences in eReaders may include features like the ability to highlight text passages and take notes. For a more detailed comparison of providers, see the Digital Providers page.
